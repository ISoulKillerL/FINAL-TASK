Music

Music means everything to me — I absolutely love it and listen to it 24/7. Whether I’m sleeping, eating, walking, or doing anything at all, there’s always music playing in the background.

Most of the time, you’ll find at least one earbud in my ear, blasting my favorite tracks at full volume. Music keeps me focused, inspired, and emotionally connected to the world around me. It’s more than just sound — it’s a part of who I am.
